What happens if someone calls or texts while my phone is inside?

While the phone is sealed inside the pouch, it is temporarily unreachable by normal wireless signals. A phone call may go to voicemail or show as unavailable, depending on the caller's carrier and your phone setup. Texts, chats, and app notifications usually wait until the phone reconnects.

FAQ

Questions before you order?

Do I need to turn off my phone first?

No. You can leave your phone as-is. The pouch is designed so you do not need to change settings like silent, vibrate, or airplane mode.

Will my phone ring inside the pouch?

If the pouch is properly closed and blocking the signal as intended, the phone should not receive the incoming call in real time. Depending on your carrier and phone setup, the call may go to voicemail or appear as unavailable.

Will I get my texts and notifications afterward?

Usually, messages and notifications arrive after the phone reconnects, but exact behavior depends on the app, phone, carrier, and how long the phone was disconnected.

Does this work better than silent mode?

Silent mode stops sound. Kavanah Pouch creates a physical separation from the phone and is designed to block incoming wireless signals, so the phone is not actively receiving interruptions while inside.

What phones fit?

Kavanah Pouch fits most standard smartphones. Final fit dimensions will be updated after sample testing.
