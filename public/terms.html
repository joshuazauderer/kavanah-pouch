Product Description

Kavanah Pouch is a signal-blocking phone pouch designed to block wireless signals when properly closed, helping reduce distractions during davening. Signal-blocking performance depends on proper closure, device type, carrier, and environment. Results may vary.

The pouch is designed to block normal wireless signals (cellular, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth) while properly sealed. We do not guarantee 100% signal blocking in all situations, with all devices, or in all environments.
